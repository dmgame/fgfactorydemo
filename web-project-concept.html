Orks Village

Concept art

Promonation art design, concept art of characters, props and buildings. Characters 2d animation using Spine.

Every concept art is a story imagined and visualized by an artist. This story was imagined on a classic fantasy world of Warcraft basis and designed in a casual way. Main character concept art stage included different shapes and ideas of the main warrior, his emotions and poses. Later stages included pets design, colors research and final rendering.
