Game models and renders

We offer the most efficient solutions based on your the technical requirements, time and budget. Our process is optimized to, includes the contribution of 2d and 3d artists; our team covers all types of technologies, approaches and software tools to create 3d game assets of different complexity, style and usage. We offer dedicated 3d specialists or fully staffed remote teams up to 10 people comprising of an art lead, artists, animators and technical support.

  • In-game assets
  • 3d renders for game purposes

Over the last few years we have released a great number of projects that are connected with 3d graphics. Our assets are used for various types of products that require 3d graphics: 3d mobile game assets, graphics for desktop and VR games, videos and commercials, 360 degrees panoramas and promotional materials. We cover different 3d graphics styles creating 3d models for video games in casual, realistic and hand painted textures.

Our 3d artists work closely with developers. Having the team of Unity developers in-house, we also control the fate of Unity 3d game assets. You can find more information about our development process here.
