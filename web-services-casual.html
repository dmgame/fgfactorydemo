Casual game graphics

We believe that delivering a WOW effect for the end user is definitely a must during each casual game design. During the initial stage of mobile game graphics design, we discover and analyze what mobile users like, and then we create the mixture of colors, shapes, light and motion to ensure that the right results are delivered. We follow this approach when creating 2d game assets for mobile and desktop casual games, promo assets and motion 2d graphics.

Based on the game idea and mechanics we offer a wide variety of game design and graphics features and details.

Fgfactory offers both individual artists and complete art teams up to 10 specialists comprising of an art lead, 2d artists, designers and motion artists. Our management can build your design team from scratch or our people can strengthen your existing team.

We cover all major platforms and adapt to any specialized requests. If you require an exclusive iOS game graphics design services, we are able to offer the right staff and make specific project research based mobile experience, trends and target platform. If you require non-mobile computer game graphics design services, a research will be conducted focusing on desktop, different audience and user experience.
