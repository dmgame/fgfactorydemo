Meet the team

Fgfactory features a team of experienced artists, creative designers, developers, testers, technical managers and game producers. We are the mixed potion of creative, smart and ambitious people who work together to deliver great products.

We pay great attention to both design and internal implementation of our solutions. We manage the process using a smart mix of methodologies to create a zero-fat productive development process and to deliver high quality products. Using Unity development technologies we create amazing game products for all platforms that it supports and covers all customer targets and requirements.

Today we are a software development agency that specializes in custom game development for Mobile, Apple TV, Mac, Windows and VR and art production for mobile and video games with the development center in Kharkiv, Ukraine.

We always welcome new people in our team: cool developers and game designers, artists and managers. We have fun and a lot of interesting work surrounded by truly talented people. Take a look at our current opening positions.
